  • Configuration:
  • Fetch and data preferences:

  • This saves your selections on the browser's local storage everytime you make a change. This can be taxing to your browser! Disable it if you are experiencing slowdowns.

  • 1.25 sec.

    This adds an artificial delay (in seconds) to every request made to GitHub, to prevent issues with their rate limit. You can reduce it to increase the speed and responsiveness of the packer, with the added risk of getting "Too Many Requests (429)" errors.

  • Export/Import settings and selections:

  • Export: only in .z64packersettings format, which is a json file with no binaries. Always included in every song pack zip.
    Import: available in .z64packersettings and .zip format. The zip is automatically searched for a z64packersettings file, and also compares the actual files; it may take a while to process.
